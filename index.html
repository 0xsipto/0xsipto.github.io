Our Core Values

Core value #1

Decentralized & Collaborative

Owned and granting power to the people, rather than a central authority. We believe greatness can only be achieved if we promote by giving forward and in an very open and collaborative enviornment.

Core value #2

Democracy & Integrity

We believe in the power of the people, and democratising and accessibility will eventually lead to progress. We have honesty and respect for all individuals and demand it from others.

Core value #3

Purposeful & Value Driven

We are an organization driven to provide and extend the highest form of value and extract the same. We are building with a purpose of - “Democratising Investing for all”

Core value #4

Unbiased

We are radically open minded and transparent  about what’s right and truly commit to understanding the reasoning with the people we disagree with.

Core value #5

Biased for Action

Speed matters, Many decisions and actions are reversible and do not need extensive study. We value calculated risk-taking and would always favour action for inaction.
